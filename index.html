Engineering Course

Toulouse INP - ENSEEIHT (N7)

Founded in 1907, ENSEEIHT (or "N7") is a top-tier French engineering school within the Toulouse INP, located in the heart of Toulouse.

Its core mission is to train engineers to meet the grand challenges of the digital revolution and the energy transition.

My Path: Energy, Electronics & Automation

My Department: 3EA

I am currently a second-year student (M1 equivalent) in the "Energy, Electronics, and Automation" (3EA) department. My specialization is in **Energy**, where I study the principles of electrical energy production, conversion, and management.

My Goal: The ACISE Track

My objective is to join the **ACISE** (Architecture de Commande et Informatique des Systèmes Embarqués) track for my S8 and S9 semesters. This specialization is at the intersection of real-time control, embedded systems, and industrial automation.

Focus: ACISE Specialization

Core Competencies

Real-Time Automation

Analysis of complex systems, software/hardware modeling, and real-time control algorithms.

Embedded & Critical Systems

Design of algorithms for decision-making, piloting, and ensuring functional safety and security.

System Mastery

Control of continuous-state (robots, machines) and discrete-event (energy management) systems.

Key Application Domains

Smart Grids & Energy Transition
Robotics & New Mobilities
Embedded & Critical Systems
