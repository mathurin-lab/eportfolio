Engineering Course

Toulouse INP - ENSEEIHT (N7)

Founded in 1907, ENSEEIHT (or "N7") is a top-tier French engineering school within the Toulouse INP, located in the heart of Toulouse.

Its core mission is to train engineers to meet the grand challenges of the digital revolution and the energy transition.
